Gesture Control

  • Hannah Moon
    • Gesture Signal
    • Touchless Control

'Gesture Control' offers easily manipulating functions without touching the screen to users. Using gestures mean that you can now control functions simply by waving in the air without touching the screen directly, so it is easier and faster to operate than touch screens and buttons. Manipulating or controlling functions with gestures would bring a new design paradigm as our lifestyle has changed by the pandemic. I want to continue researching these functions in the future.
