Wearable Technology

  • Hannah Moon
    • Future Computer

The era of smartphones is fundamentally changing our lives. A desktop computer on the table has moved into the palm of your hand. The fact is wearing a smart device is more convenient to than carrying a phone around. Now the era is changing "from mobile to wear." More and more wearable computers create a better user experience and I will explore wearable computer technology and the future wearable digital revolution.

“Can wearable devices be helpful? Absolutely. But they can also be horrid. It all depends upon whether we use them to focus and augment our activities or to distract. It is up to us, and up to those who create these new wearable wonders to decide which it is to be.”
by Don Normal on July 24, 2013

Project Jacquard by Google
'Jacquard' is one of the projects released from Google. The 'Jacquard' is a project to study smart cloth. After weaving the fabric with conductive threads, the clothes allow you to operate your smart device. For example, you can answer the phone or play music with different hand gestures.
