Hello!


I am Hannah Moon, a creative leader who strives to create a better user experience. I can envision and design integral solutions by utilizing design methodologies, different technologies, techniques, and interactive data visualization to deliver optimal and delightful user experiences.





What's in my design tool box?

I utilize different tools to frame ambiguous problemes and transform data into creative solutions. Using modern technologies and techniques help me to see problems from a holistic view point while putting users at the center of solving problems.



Research

I conduct A to Z user research, including interviews, contextual inquiry, quantitative and qualitative research, survey design and usability testings to reveal user's core needs and transform them into development opportunities.

Design

I am strong at creating ambient user interactive products or services by utilizing modern tools. I use Figma, InVision, Adobe Cloud, and Azure for a rapid proto typing. I use JIRA, Slack, and Trello for managing products.

Coding

I am familiar with R and Tableau and use them for data analysis and data visualization. I create front-end interaction with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I also have experience in writing Python.

Analysis

I analyze data and incorporate insights into my design practice. Data analysis helps me to draw logical user flows, identify product development opportunities, and evaluate the success of my designs.
